Profile

My name is Haodong Liu, currently a senior majored in software engineering at Tongji University, Shanghai, China.

Besides course projects of school, I had lots of project experiences including mobile application, (especially android development), game development, and data processing and analysis by using R language.

My future plan is to gain more research experiences in my graduate school and even pursue a P.H.D degree, specializing in mobile computing area.

Another two fields I think pretty hot are distributed system and data science, I will keep my eye on these and desire much to be involved in.

My last intern was at Microsoft Research Asia, System and Algorithm group. I joined in a geomagnetic-based indoor localization research project. It made me rethink and gained more confidence to challenge myself in the computer world.
