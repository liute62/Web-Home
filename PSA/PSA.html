Synopsis

Tongji University Vehicle Interactive Platform aims to build a innovative and robust nature human interface and user behavior testing platform based on a real car.

The platform carries Three-Screen Interactive Information and Entertainment System (high-dimension screen controlcenter, digital dashboard and windshield projection), Sandbox and Cross Analysis testing System(user behavior).

The platform supports gestures, voices and other related natural human-computer interaction method, which makes room for future intelligent embedded product, drivers' personalization and construction of user data accumulation and analysis platform.
